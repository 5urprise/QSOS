Changelog :
    +    +    +    +    +

[QSOS Formats]
* XSD for QSOS format
* Freemind XSD for templates

[QSOS backend]
* QSOS backend is now split in two
    + Incoming: community sandbox opened to any registered user
    + Master: QSOS certified evaluations and templates
* Templates and evaluations can be uploaded to the backends, this requires authentication

[QSOS Editor]
* Remote Templates can be downloaded from the QSOS backend and used to create a new evaluation
* Remote evaluations can be downloaded from the QSOS backends and opened by the editor
* Evaluations can be send and saved in the remote QSOS backends, this requires authentication
* Evaluations can be resynchronized with local or remote templates

[03S]
* 03S connects to a QSOS by configuration
* Internationalization of GUI, icluding evaluations filtering on language
* Evaluations can now be visualized in HTML and Freemind format
* Comparisons can now be visualized on a QSOS Quadrant and also be exported to OpenDocument (ODS, ODP and ODT formats)
